The Festival
Five days. One spirit.

Ganeshotsav at IIT Guwahati is more than a celebration — it is a living tradition that unites students, faculty, and the community in devotion and joy. Every year we welcome Lord Ganesha with grandeur, music, and a spirit that transforms the entire campus.
